Talent Benefits Hiring
Talent

Tap into an extraordinary group of designers.


Our intensive 10 week program is custom-built to produce designers who are ready for the working world. Hiring Partners get access to our unique talent pool. Here’s a little bit of what it takes to make the grade at DESIGNATION:

Selective admissions process

Only the brightest and most passionate individuals who demonstrate exceptional ability are accepted into the program. Once accepted, each student must undertake more than 60 hours of prep work before the first class session begins.

600+ hours of immersion

Throughout our 10 week program, all students must spend at least sixty hours a week learning through lectures, collaborating in teams, and building a killer portfolio — all in a vibrant, design-focused environment.

Full-stack curriculum

From UX research to front-end web development, our alumni go through a rigorous digital design education. They learn practical design and problem-solving skills from real world projects, all under the direction of real working professionals.

Process-oriented education

Our students take projects all the way from scratch to deployment. They have the knowledge and skills to become effective team members that add value from day one.

Benefits

Find better talent faster.


Make your talent search more efficient. As a Hiring Partner, you’ll get first crack at a pool of talented designers who have already proven they have what it takes. Here are just a few of the benefits:

All-In-One Student Database

Browse detailed profiles of potential applicants. See real work completed in the DESIGNATION program, and run filtered searches to quickly identify the perfect candidate.

Private Job Board

Create as many or as few job listings as you want, free of charge. Choose from full-time, internship, or contract work. Tap into our entire network of students, alumni and instructors.

Guest Speaking

Hiring Partners are always welcome to host guest lectures at any time. In addition to providing a valuable resource for students, it’s also the perfect way to make introductions and get an early look at fresh talent.

Portfolio Night

See the results DESIGNATION generates for yourself. Portfolio Night is an open showcase for students to demonstrate everything they’ve learned, and meet potential employers.


Becoming a Hiring Partner, and see for yourself why DESIGNATION is the premier source for new talent in digital design.
