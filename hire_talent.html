Alumni

Tap into an extraordinary group of designers.


Our intense ten-week program in Chicago focuses specifically on producing great designers. Hiring Partners have exclusive access to our unique talent pool of brilliant graduates. Our alumni have accomplished some impressive feats:

Selective admissions process

Only the brightest and most passionate group of individuals, who genuinely love design, are accepted into the program. Once accepted, each student must undertake more than fifty hours of prep work before the first class session.

600+ hours of design immersion

All students spend at least sixty hours a week, for ten weeks, learning design through lectures, collaborating in teams, and building up portfolio work — all in a vibrant, design-focused environment.

Full stack curriculum

From UX research to front-end web development, our alumni go through a rigorous and thorough digital design education. They learn practical design and problem-solving skills that center around real-world projects.

Complete design process

Our students understand what it takes to design user-centered experiences from scratch to deployment. They have the knowledge and skills to become effective team members that add value from day one.

Benefits

Hire remarkable designers without the hassle.


Be more efficient when it comes to recruiting. From start to finish, we've made your search for design talent as seamless as possible. When you become a Hiring Partner, you get access to the complete list of benefits:

Detailed Student Database

Browse a complete list of graduate profiles with links to portfolio work and online presence. Filter searches specifically targeting the type of designer you're looking for. Only contact the candidates you want to interview — all at no cost.

Private Job Board

List unlimited design jobs for free, anytime. Post full-time positions, internships, or contract work. The entire Designation network of alumni, instructors, and mentors have access to this exclusive job board.

Guest Speaking Presentations

We hold several guest presentations throughout each cohort in order to provide opportunities for you to meet our students face-to-face during the program. Hiring Partners have first access in reserving guest speaking slots.

Sponsored Workshops & Events

Sponsoring special workshops and events allows your company to build rapport with our students. Meet and work with potential hires, and get to know them on an interpersonal level. Hiring Partners have first priority in sponsoring workshops and events.

Portfolio Night

Make introductions with an entire cohort of students, all in one night. Portfolio Night is a unique event that brings together graduates and companies, allowing our alumni a platform to showcase their hard work and detail their experiences from the last ten weeks.

Hiring

Zero upfront costs. Zero risk.


It's 100% free to become a Hiring Partner. We only charge a placement fee when you hire one of our graduates.
